Insights

  1. The home side has won all three meetings between Juventus and Bayer Leverkusen in the Champions League, with the Old Lady winning their two home games against the German side by a combined score of 7-0.
  2. Bayer Leverkusen failed to attempt a single shot on target in this match -this is the first time they have done so in a champions Legue outing since Opta began recording this data in 2003-04, in what is their 48th game played in the competition in this time.
  3. Bayer Leverkusen have become the 33rd different club against which Juventus' Cristiano Ronaldo has scored in the Champions League, equalling the record previously set by Raul ( also 33 sides).

Comments

  1. Match played at Juventus Stadium. Juventus gets their first victory in Group D, a 3-0 sucess over Bayer Leverkusen, courtesy of goals from Higuain, Bernardeschi and Ronaldo. They led 1-0 at the break, after Higuain put them ahead after 17 minutes. Juve then dominated the second hald, Bernardeschi doubling their lead early on, before Ronaldo had a couple of chances to ectend their lead, but was denied by Hradecky. However, he finally got his goal late on to make it 3-0 and wrap up victory.
